Meet Our Founder

Priya Sharma

With a deep passion for preserving traditional food techniques and promoting sustainable agriculture, Priya founded Sundried in 2010 after leaving her corporate career. Growing up in a farming family in rural Maharashtra, she witnessed firsthand the decline of traditional preservation methods and the rise of chemical preservatives.

Determined to revive these ancient practices, Priya began working with local farmers to create sun-dried products that maintained both nutritional value and authentic flavors. What started as a small operation with five farmers has now grown into a community of over 200 organic farmers across India.

"Our mission goes beyond creating delicious products. We're preserving cultural heritage, supporting sustainable farming, and bringing the purest flavors of nature to your table." - Priya Sharma
